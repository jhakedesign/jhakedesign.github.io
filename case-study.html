Case Study · Brand & Curriculum Design

Project
CREATE

A design-education curriculum built from the ground up for high school students across southern Louisiana — teaching real Adobe workflows through real client briefs.

Role
Creative Manager
Timeline
2017 – 2020
Client
PerXuade / XP Synergy
Deliverables
Curriculum, Brand Kit, Print

Overview

XP Synergy needed a program that could take students with zero design background and get them producing client-ready work within a semester. I rebuilt the curriculum from the syllabus up, pairing every lesson with a live local-business brief and a matching brand kit — type pairing, color story, and layout grids — so students learned Illustrator and InDesign the same way a junior designer would: under a deadline, with a client waiting.
